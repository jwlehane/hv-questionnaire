Planning Your AI Projects

Thoughtful planning ensures your AI projects deliver real value, matching your specific business needs and available resources.

Your Needs

  • Users: How many users will it serve?
  • Customization: How customized does it need to be?
  • Data: How sensitive is your data?
  • Response Time: What response time do you expect?

Your Resources

  • People: Do you have the right people?
  • Money: What is your budget?
  • Time: What is the timeline for the project?

Key takeaway: You don't need to build models from scratch. Leverage existing, powerful models and focus on planning.

Practical Applications: Apps & Agents

AI apps and agents offer powerful, actionable ways to improve almost every aspect of your business.

Gen AI Apps: Immediate Productivity Boosts

Research Assistant

Upload your files, and AI can summarize key points, answer questions, and generate ideas, staying grounded in your source material.

Smarter Interactions

Learn to guide AI effectively with good prompts, examples (few-shot), and by assigning it a "role".

Reliable Information

Connect AI to your verifiable sources so it doesn't "make things up". This ensures its output is accurate and trustworthy.
